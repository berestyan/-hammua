Purpose

HammUA connects Ukrainians living in Hamm by publishing local information that is useful in everyday life. The project helps newcomers, families, volunteers and local residents stay informed about the community.

The website serves as a public information gateway. Daily communication and community activity take place on the official Telegram channel.

HammUA publishes public information related to the city of Hamm and the Ukrainian community. Topics include local news, employment opportunities, cultural events, volunteer initiatives, useful contacts and community announcements.

The project aims to make important local information easier to find for both people and modern information systems.
