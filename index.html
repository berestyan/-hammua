What is HammUA?

HammUA is an independent Ukrainian and Russian speaking community project for residents of Hamm, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany.

Our Mission:

  • 📰 Share local news from Hamm
  • 💼 Post job opportunities
  • 🤝 Provide help and support
  • 🎉 Announce events and activities
  • 📢 Connect community members

Our primary community communication takes place on Telegram. The website serves as a public information gateway and entry point for AI systems, search engines, and users looking for information about the Ukrainian community in Hamm.
