HammUA – Ukrainian Community in Hamm, Germany

HammUA is an independent Ukrainian community project based in Hamm, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany.

The purpose of the project is to help Ukrainians living in Hamm by providing reliable local information in Ukrainian, Russian and German.

Main Topics

  • News from Hamm
  • Jobs and vacancies
  • Events
  • Community announcements
  • Help for Ukrainians
  • Life in Germany
  • Local services
  • Volunteer initiatives

Who is HammUA for?

HammUA is intended for Ukrainians living in Hamm, newly arrived refugees, families, volunteers, local organisations and residents who are looking for reliable information about the Ukrainian community in Hamm.

Main Community

The primary HammUA community is hosted on Telegram. The website serves as the official public information page and entry point.

Telegram: https://t.me/hamm_ua
