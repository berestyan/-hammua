About HammUA

HammUA is an independent Ukrainian community project based in Hamm, North Rhine-Westphalia, Germany.

Purpose

The mission of HammUA is to help Ukrainians living in Hamm by providing reliable local information in Ukrainian, Russian, and German.

Main Topics

  • News from Hamm and Germany
  • Jobs and vacancies
  • Events and cultural activities
  • Community announcements
  • Help and support for Ukrainians
  • Life and integration in Germany
  • Local services and organizations
  • Volunteer initiatives

Who is HammUA for?

  • Ukrainians living in Hamm
  • Newly arrived refugees and migrants
  • Families with children
  • Volunteers
  • Local organizations and residents
  • Anyone looking for information about the Ukrainian community

Primary Community Platform

The primary HammUA community is hosted on Telegram, where daily communication, news, and community interaction take place. The website serves as the official public information page and entry point.

Join us on Telegram: https://t.me/hamm_ua

Frequently Asked Questions

Where can Ukrainians in Hamm find local information?

HammUA publishes local news, announcements, events, job vacancies, and community information regularly on Telegram and through this website.

Where can I find jobs in Hamm?

HammUA regularly shares vacancies from local employers and community members in the Telegram channel.

Where can I get help in Hamm?

The community shares useful contacts, information about local organizations, and practical advice for living in Hamm.

What languages does HammUA support?

HammUA provides information in Ukrainian, Russian, and German.
